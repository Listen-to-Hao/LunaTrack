Welcome to LunaTrack

Care about menstrual problems Care about women's health

Record and analyze full text information

Our platform provides female users with a convenient tool for recording and tracking retrospective cycles. Through intelligent algorithms, you can not only easily enter the start and end dates of the retrospective, but also get statistical analysis and trend charts of the cycle. The system will provide you with accurate predictions based on your personal data to help you better manage your health.

Menstrual health analysis and suggestions

Combined with your recorded menstrual data, our analysis function provides a detailed health report covering multiple health indicators such as cycle regularity and menstrual symptoms. The platform will provide personalized suggestions and reminders based on the analysis results to help you stay healthy, detect possible health problems in time, and avoid unnecessary worries.

Women's Health Community

We are committed to creating a platform for support and sharing for female users. In the community, you can post about health, menstruation, lifestyle, etc., and share experiences and insights with other women. This is a warm and safe space that encourages everyone to support each other and pay attention to and improve women's health.
